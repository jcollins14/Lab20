Travel

I love to travel. I tend to travel to large cities. I've all over the United States, from New York to Seattle. I am currently planning on going to Japan for my first international trip in October 2020 (barring any COVID-19 worsening over the next six months). Personally, I feel travel is the best way to get out and learn about people and cultures that differ from your own, expanding your perspective and allowing you to empathize with others more.

Food

I'm a big Foodie, and make it the focus of wherever I travel. I picked up an interest in cooking from watching the Food Network after school when I was a child. I learned how to properly cook from an old roommate and good friend of mine who is a culinary school graduate and established chef. I find it very enjoyable to cook for those I care about.
